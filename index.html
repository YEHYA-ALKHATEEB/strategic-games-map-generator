Strategy Games Map Generator

How to Use:

  • Click "Add Banner" to add a 1x1 banner.
  • Click "Add Spot" to add a 2x2 spot.
  • Click "Add Bear" to add a 3x3 bear.
  • Double-click any shape to rename it.
  • Change Color: Right-click on a shape and type "color" in the prompt to change its color.
  • Delete Shape: Right-click on a shape and type "delete" in the prompt, or select the shape and press the Delete key.
  • Use zoom buttons to zoom in/out, and scroll to navigate.
  • Click "Save Map" to save your work, and "Load Map" to restore it later.
